About

Welcome to Pit Stop – the ultimate destination for a food and fun adventure! Nestled in the heart of Clapham, we're your go-to spot for a lively dining and drinking experience.

Our outdoor oasis invites you to soak up the sun while savouring a variety of flavors. From mouthwatering pizzas and juicy burgers to zesty tacos, there's a little something for everyone. And when it comes to drinks, we've got it all – from wine to beer and expertly crafted cocktails.

No matter the weather, our versatile venue has you covered. Enjoy the fresh air at our outdoor seating, or seek shelter under our expansive tents with outdoor heating during the cooler months. Inside, our bars are buzzing with energy, offering you the perfect place to unwind.

Just like our food and drinks, Pit Stop is all about a good time. So gather your friends, make new ones, and join us for an unforgettable experience.
